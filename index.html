about

Justin Kord is a Seattle-raised and Miami-based singer, songwriter, producer and multi-instrumentalist. She is currently studying in the Bruce Hornsby Creative American Music singer-songwriter program at the Frost School of Music, University of Miami.

Her music blends the sounds of 80's synth pop with elements of today’s hit songs. She tells personal stories through visceral lyrics that paint colorful mental images. Alison’s sound can be compared to 80's artists such as Tears for Fears and the Eurythmics, as well as modern artists, such as Lorde, Dagny, SG Lewis and Clairo.

$ire's New Single, Let You Go, was released in September, 2018. You can find it on all platforms.

$ire's Background

Raised in a musical household and surrounded by a wide variety of music from a young age, Alison began singing almost before she could talk. She started playing classical piano at age 5, later learning guitar, bass and jazz piano. For the past 6 years, she has been writing songs and producing electronic music, shaped by the unique high school original composition music program at the Bush School under the tutelage of Jill Wangsgard. Alison has written over 40 songs across a variety of genres including pop, electro pop, ballad, rock, R&B, blues, country and singer-songwriter. She has performed for years at various venues in the US and Europe including cafés, open mics, parties and street fairs, both solo and with a band.

$ire is influenced by many genres including 80’s alternative, pop music, New Wave, dream and bedroom pop, EDM, Motown, singer-songwriter, classical and jazz. Some of her favorite artists include Lorde, Taylor Swift, Ariana Grande, Sara Bareilles, San Holo, Prince, New Order, The Cure, Aretha Franklin, and Carole King.
